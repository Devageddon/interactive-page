Introduction

At UNB, we are future-focused. We know that education and research are the cornerstones of informed decision making and innovation. We also know that industry partnership, coupled with rich community engagement, are key to success. Through connection, collaboration and formal partnership agreements, UNB has become a leader in the provision of applied education and research that addresses the needs of industry and its workforce.
